Welcome. We've been meaning to put together a proper family site for a while now, and here we finally are.

We're Dustin and Maggie, living in San Francisco with our two kids — Noah and Emilia. This site is meant to be a simple, unhurried place to share what we're up to: little notes on everyday life, places we've explored, and moments worth writing down.

San Francisco has been home for a few years now, and we've grown to love its particular rhythms: the fog rolling in over Twin Peaks in the evening, weekend mornings at Dolores Park, the way the city always has something new to discover around the next corner.

Noah arrived in February 2023 and turned our world upside down in the best possible way. Emilia came along in February 2025, and now our apartment feels wonderfully chaotic and full.

We're not going for perfection here — just presence. A record of the small and the significant, written for ourselves and for anyone who cares to read along.

Thanks for stopping by.
